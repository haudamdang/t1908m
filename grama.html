NOUNS


A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KING OF NOUNS


Nouns are classified as followes

Proper:It is the name of particular object, whether person place, animal ,idea of thing

Common:It is the nam possessed by things in general

Collecive:It is the name representing a group of things

Abstract::It is the name representing qualities,emotions,coditions or action


PRONOUS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid reepetition of a noun.

KINGS OF PRONOUN

Pronous are classified as follows:

Personal pronoun: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.

Interrogative pronouns :It stands as the answer to the question about a person,place or a thing.

Indefinite pronouns:It is user to refer to a person,thing or place that are unknown

Relative pronouns:It relates to a noun pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.

Demonstrative pronouns:A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.

Reflexive pronouns:It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject
